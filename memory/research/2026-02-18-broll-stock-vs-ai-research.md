# B-Roll Research: Stock vs AI-Generated for Short-Form Marketing Videos
### 2026-02-18

## Key Decisions
[decision] For personalized outreach videos at scale, hybrid approach wins: Pexels (free) + Storyblocks ($30/mo annual) for stock, OpusClip ($29/mo) or Submagic ($23/mo) for AI-assisted editing, Runway/Kling for custom AI generation when needed.

## Stock Platforms Ranked
[research]
1. **Pexels** - Free, CC0, 4K, commercial use. First stop for generic b-roll.
2. **Artgrid** - $25-50/mo annual. Cinematic quality. Lifetime use of downloads even after cancel.
3. **Storyblocks** - $30/mo annual ($360/yr). Unlimited downloads. Largest library 1.5M+ items.
4. **Envato Elements** - $16.50/mo annual. 22M+ assets including video. Good value bundle.
5. **Shutterstock/Getty** - Per-clip pricing $150-400/clip. Too expensive for scale.

## AI B-Roll Tools
[research]
- **OpusClip** - $29/mo Pro. Best for repurposing long-form to shorts with auto b-roll.
- **Submagic** - $23/mo Pro. Integrates Storyblocks library. Good for captions + b-roll.
- **Jupitrr** - $10.80-30/mo. Specialized for talking-head b-roll enhancement.
- **Runway** - $95/mo unlimited. Best quality AI generation. Good for custom scenes.
- **Kling AI** - $6.99-25.99/mo. Cheaper AI generation alternative.

## Gotchas
[gotcha] AI b-roll still bad at: hand-object interactions, typing, cooking close-ups. Use stock for these.
[gotcha] Storyblocks customer support reportedly terrible per Reddit. Artgrid better experience.
[gotcha] Runway "Unlimited" plan still requires credits for higher quality outputs. Misleading name.
[gotcha] AI clips need multiple generations + cherry-picking. Budget 3-5x generation time.

## Cost Math for Scale
[research]
- Stock-only (Storyblocks): $360/yr unlimited
- AI-only (Runway Pro): $1,140/yr
- Hybrid recommended: Pexels free + OpusClip $348/yr = ~$350/yr
- Per-clip stock (Getty/Shutterstock): $150-400/clip = unusable at scale
- Traditional video production: $10K-50K per 60-second video = completely unusable
