# AI Marketing Colleague Research - 2026-02-06

## Summary
Deep research into what the most advanced AI employee/agent systems look like when acting as proactive business colleagues, specifically for small business marketing.

## Key Findings

### Proactive AI Systems
- Google's CC agent (Dec 2025): delivers "Your Day Ahead" briefing unprompted
- GrubMarket's Monitoring AI Agent: detects and alerts critical business issues proactively
- By 2026, AI agents embedded in 80% of enterprise apps, making 15% of work decisions autonomously
- Shift from LLMs to LAMs (Large Action Models) - systems that do things, not just chat

### The Jacob Bank / Relay.app Model (Most Relevant)
- Solo founder runs million-dollar business with 40 AI agents, zero employees
- Agents organized by marketing channel: social, blog, email, lead qualification
- Scheduled cadences: competitive research Fridays, content ideas Saturdays, support summaries Mondays
- Costs: few hundred $/month in API costs
- Human-in-the-loop: reviews before shipping, like managing interns
- Key: "research -> create -> engage -> learn" loop compressed from days to hours

### SaaStr / Jason Lemkin Model
- 20 AI agents replaced 10 human SDRs/AEs
- Managed by 1.2 humans
- Sends 70,000 hyper-personalized emails vs 7,000 before
- Generated 15% of SaaStr London's revenue
- Key insight: "Training's more important than the tool"

### Marketing Frameworks in AI
- Hormozi's Value Equation encoded into AI prompt chains works well
- AI more consistent at applying frameworks than humans (addresses 27 objection categories vs human's 5-7)
- Implementation: prompt chaining / mega-prompts, not fine-tuning
- Custom GPTs and system prompts encoding StoryBrand, $100M Offers, etc.

### Technical Architecture
- Agentic RAG: agents + retrieval-augmented generation for domain knowledge
- Top frameworks 2026: LangChain, LangGraph, AutoGen, n8n, Relay.app
- n8n: 2,424 marketing workflow templates, drag-and-drop AI agents
- Domain specialization via: RAG pipelines, system prompts, task decomposition

### Restaurant/Bar Specific
- SpotOn Marketing Assist: automated email + social for restaurants
- Popmenu: AI marketing for restaurants
- Dishio AI: personalized guest messaging based on visit patterns
- AI phone agents for reservation management

### Competitive Intelligence for Local Business
- Crayon: launched MCP server for embedding CI into Claude/ChatGPT
- ForumScout: monitors Reddit, X, LinkedIn with natural language filtering
- Visualping: tracks competitor website/pricing changes
- Feedly Leo: AI-powered market intelligence

### What's Actually Working (2025-2026)
- Solo marketers managing 12 retainers at $750-$1,000 each with <$500/month tools
- AI automating 70-80% of agency operations
- 150-300% productivity gains
- Content marketing: first revenue at month 3, significant growth at month 8-9
- Paid marketing costs down 30% with AI targeting
