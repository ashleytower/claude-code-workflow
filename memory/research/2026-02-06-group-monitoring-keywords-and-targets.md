## CAMPAIGN 2: Managed Bar Program (B2B - Owners Without Craft Bartenders)

### The Gap
Cocktail bar-quality drinks that anybody can make. Owners are overwhelmed managing their bar.
MTL Craft provides: syrups, garnishes, easy recipes, consistency. Eventually an inventory system.
The buyer is the OWNER, not the bartender. Target venues that want great cocktails but don't have
(or can't keep) a dedicated mixologist. "Let us handle your cocktail program so you don't have to."

### Competitors in This Space
- WithCo Cocktails (premade mixers with botanicals, targets stadiums/hospitality groups)
- Wandering Barman (premade full cocktails, Bushwick-based, speed + consistency pitch)
- Bartesian (cocktail capsule machine, consumer-facing)
- Unfiltered Hospitality (beverage consulting for restaurants/hotels)
MTL Craft differentiator: handmade syrups not premade cocktails, MK Kosher certified,
bilingual FR/EN, local Montreal, full package (syrups + garnishes + recipes + training),
and eventually AI-powered inventory system.
